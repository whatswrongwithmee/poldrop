How It Works

From connect to claim
in four simple steps

Polydrop rewards genuine Polymarket participants. Here's exactly how eligibility is verified and how rewards reach your wallet — no seed phrase, no hidden steps.

01

Review & agree

Read our Fair Participation Policy and confirm you're a real, single-wallet participant. No Sybil farming.

02

Connect your wallet

Link MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, WalletConnect, or Phantom. We only read your public address.

03

We verify activity

Your public Polymarket history is checked against the eligibility rules and scored automatically.

04

Claim your rewards

Eligible wallets claim USDC directly on-chain. Funds land in your wallet — no middleman.

Who's eligible

Eligibility is based entirely on verifiable, on-chain participation. You meet the bar if your wallet checks these boxes.

Minimum on-chain activity

A baseline level of real trading or market participation on Polymarket.

Genuine transactions

Eligible markets and trades — not wash trading or artificial volume.

Holding history

Activity spread over time, showing real engagement rather than last-minute farming.

No Sybil flags

Your wallet isn't linked to a cluster of accounts farming the same reward pool.

Rewards & fair play

How allocations are calculated — and why we take Sybil resistance seriously.

How rewards are calculated

Your allocation scales with the depth and consistency of your real participation:

  • Volume and number of eligible trades
  • How long you've been active on Polymarket
  • Diversity of markets you've participated in
  • Overall eligibility score (0–100)

Why one wallet only

Creating many wallets to farm rewards (Sybil behavior) drains the pool from real users. We don't allow it.

  • Linked address clusters are detected and flagged
  • Sybil wallets forfeit all rewards
  • Keeping it fair means more for genuine participants

Frequently asked

Quick answers to the things people ask most.

Do I need to share my seed phrase?
Never. Connecting a wallet only shares your public address. We will never ask for your seed phrase or private keys — anyone who does is trying to scam you.
When are rewards paid out?
Once your wallet is verified as eligible, rewards become claimable on your dashboard. Claiming sends USDC directly to your wallet on-chain.
What token and network are rewards in?
Rewards are distributed in USDC, sent directly to the wallet you connect. A small network fee applies when you claim, shown transparently before you confirm.
Can I use multiple wallets?
No. Polydrop is one wallet per person. Using multiple wallets to farm rewards is Sybil activity and leads to disqualification of all linked wallets.
What data do you access?
Only your public wallet address and publicly available Polymarket activity, used to verify eligibility. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
