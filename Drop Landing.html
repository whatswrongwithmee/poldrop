Fair Participation Policy

No Sybil activity

Polydrop rewards genuine, unique participants. Creating multiple wallets or accounts to farm rewards (Sybil behavior) is strictly prohibited and will lead to disqualification.

Connect your Polymarket wallet

Connect the wallet you use on Polymarket to verify your activity

Use your Polymarket profile address (from polymarket.com/profile/…) — not your MetaMask address.
Where do I find this?
  1. Open polymarket.com and click your avatar (top-right) → Profile.
  2. Your profile URL looks like polymarket.com/profile/0x…
  3. Copy that 0x… address (or the whole link) and paste it above.

Tip: this is different from your MetaMask address — Polymarket trades live on a separate profile wallet.
